# FMCG Deal Intelligence Newsletter
### July 18, 2026

## Executive Summary
This period saw a massive wave of consolidation in the Indian FMCG sector, with major conglomerates actively pursuing premium beauty, personal care, and health food brands. Market leaders like Hindustan Unilever, Marico, and Dabur dominated transaction activity through strategic acquisitions of high-growth D2C startups and international brand expansions. Key deal highlights include multi-million dollar investments in clean beauty and health wellness segments, reflecting a broader industry-wide shift toward premiumization and lifestyle-oriented consumer trends.

## Deals This Period

### Dabur
**Dabur acquires a minority stake in RAS Beauty** — The company acquired a minority stake in the luxury skincare D2C company for Rs 60 crore to bolster its new ventures arm. Source: The Economic Times, Business Standard.

**Dabur acquires Asian Consumer Care** — The firm acquired the entire remaining stake in Asian Consumer Care from its joint venture partner to gain full ownership. Source: Business Standard.

**Dabur acquires Sesa Care** — The company expanded its haircare portfolio with this key brand acquisition. Source: livemint.com.

**Dabur's Burman family acquires stake in Eveready Industries** — The founding family increased its stake in the dry-cell battery manufacturer by purchasing an additional 8.48%. Source: livemint.com.

**Dabur acquires majority stake in Fem Care Pharma** — The personal care giant bought a majority stake in the company for Rs 204 crore. Source: The Economic Times.

### Emami
**Emami acquires Axiom Ayurveda** — The company acquired a 100% stake in Axiom Ayurveda in a deal worth up to ₹200 crore to bolster its natural wellness and ayurvedic offerings. Source: The Economic Times.

**Emami acquires Helios Lifestyle** — The firm acquired the remaining 49.6% stake in Helios Lifestyle, gaining 100% ownership of the men's grooming brand, The Man Company. Source: livemint.com.

### Marico
**Marico acquires Skinetiq** — The company's international arm acquired Vietnam-based beauty brand Skinetiq for ₹261 crore to scale its presence in Southeast Asia. Source: livemint.com.

**Marico acquires Cosmix Wellness** — Marico acquired a 60% majority stake in the plant-based nutrition brand for nearly ₹226 crore, aligning with the rising prominence of health foods in consumer diets. Source: Moneycontrol.com, livemint.com.

**Marico acquires True Elements** — The firm completed its buyout of health food brand True Elements, acquiring the remaining 46% stake after its initial 54% purchase. Source: Moneycontrol.com, The Economic Times.

**Marico acquires Fiancee** — The company expanded its overseas hair care business by acquiring Egypt's hair care brand Fiancee. Source: The Economic Times.

### Godrej Consumer Products
**Godrej Consumer Products acquires Muuchstac** — The company signed a pact to acquire the men's grooming brand in an all-cash deal worth Rs 450 crore, though the promoters will continue to run the business operations. Source: The Economic Times, Business Standard.

### Hindustan Unilever (HUL)
**Hindustan Unilever acquires Minimalist (Uprising Science)** — HUL acquired a 90.5% majority stake in the premium skincare brand for approximately Rs 2,995 crore to strengthen its premium beauty portfolio. Source: The Economic Times, livemint.com, Business Standard, Moneycontrol.com.

**Hindustan Unilever buys Zywie Ventures** — HUL bought Zywie Ventures for Rs 824 crore to expand its health and wellbeing division, while simultaneously divesting its stake in Nutritionalab. Source: Business Standard, Moneycontrol.com.

### Reliance Consumer Products
**Reliance Consumer Products acquires SIL** — The company acquired SIL (Sujata Instant Foods) to scale up its food and beverage portfolio and challenge established players like HUL and Tata. Source: Business Standard.

**Reliance Consumer Products acquires Velvette** — The retail giant acquired the domestic FMCG brand Velvette, famous for pioneering the sachet packaging revolution in India. Source: Business Standard.

### ITC
**ITC acquires Sresta Natural Bioproducts** — The conglomerate acquired the organic food company and its 24 Mantra Organic brand in a Rs 472.5 crore deal to expand its presence in the healthy foods segment. Source: The Economic Times, Business Standard.

**ITC acquires Mother Sparsh** — The company acquired a 16% stake in the D2C natural personal care brand. Source: Business Standard.

### Temasek
**Temasek acquires Haldiram’s stake** — The global investment firm acquired a 10% stake in Haldiram's snacks business for $1 billion. Source: Business Standard.

### Nestle SA
**Nestle SA acquires pet food brand stake** — The Swiss consumer goods giant marked its first direct funding in India's pet care space by buying a stake in an undisclosed domestic pet food brand. Source: Business Standard.
